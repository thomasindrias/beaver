# Your screen,
as data.

Beaver is a Mac menu-bar utility. Press **Cmd+Shift+D**, drag a box around anything you can see, and get it back as clean Markdown. Tables stay tables. Code stays code. This page is what that looks like.

Try the toggle in the top bar. Structure survives either way. That's the point.

Plain OCR is private but flattens everything. Cloud AI understands everything but is slow, metered, and reads a shrunk copy of your screenshot. Beaver sits in the gap: understanding at OCR speed, with OCR privacy.

## If you feed an AI, read this part

Sending a screenshot to a model feels free. It isn't, and it's lossy in ways you don't see:

  • Models bill images by pixel count. A full-window screenshot runs roughly **2,000 tokens**. The table inside it, as Markdown, is usually under 200.
  • Large screenshots get downscaled before the model reads them. Small text drops below what the encoder can resolve.
  • The model doesn't say "I can't read this digit". It guesses, and a guessed 7 looks exactly like a real 7. In one published benchmark, GPT-4V got 35 of 50 table questions wrong.
> Beaver crops the exact region you choose and reads it at full resolution, on your Mac. Your model gets exact text at a tenth of the tokens. Better input, better answers, smaller bill.

## How it works

  1. Press ⌘⇧D. A capture overlay appears over any app, video, or call.
  2. Drag a box around the thing you need. Just the table, just the code.
  3. A local vision model turns it into Markdown and copies it. History is saved to a SQLite file on your disk.

## Nothing leaves your Mac

The vision model runs on your machine: MLX on Apple Silicon, llama.cpp on Intel. A capture has no network path, which means the privacy claim is an architecture, not a policy. That matters for the documents you'd never paste into a chatbot: invoices, contracts, patient notes, anything under NDA.

  • Open source, MIT licensed. Read it, build it yourself.
  • The only network call is an optional update check against GitHub. One environment variable turns it off.
  • No account, no subscription, no meter. Works offline, works on a plane.

## Install

Download the DMG, drag Beaver to Applications, grant screen recording once. The model downloads on first launch, then everything runs offline.
